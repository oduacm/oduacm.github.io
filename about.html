About ACM

This is what we do.

ACM or Association for Computing Machinery is an organization that promotes both networking and collaboration amongst students and professionals in technology fields

As a member of our club you will have opportunities to network with fellow students, get career advice from professionals in the field, and participate in programming activities.

There is no membership fee to participate as an ACM Member. This club is open to anyone who would like to learn more about the field of technology. It doesn't matter what your major is, you are welcome here.
